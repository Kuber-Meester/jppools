About Us

We are a family owned and operated business established in 2006 based in the East Rand Gauteng. We were very excited to open our first retail store in 2023 so that we could accommodate the change in how our customers needed to be serviced. With over 23 years of experience, we strive to provide the best possible products and services at reasonable prices. Our dedicated and skilled crew can build your new dream pool or pond but can also apply The JP Pool and aquatic solutions magic to transform your existing pool and pond from DRAB to FAB!
